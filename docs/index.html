What's my balance sheet today?

Whether you are holding Bitcoin, Ethereum or hundreds of altcoins, you want to know how much money is allocated to each crypto asset at all times.

  • Track balances and prices of specific cryptocurrencies in the portfolio
  • Get updates on final balance of your portfolio
  • Support for US Dollar, Euro, Chinese Yuan, Japanese Yen, Indian Rupee and all cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, etc..

What's my portfolio history?

Your portfolio's value change as crypto is doing what it does best - being volatile. Get insights into how it all "stacks up" over time on one chart.

  • Track how your portfolio is performing over time
  • Select different time spans for more perspective
  • Get insights into what are the biggest gainers and losers over time
  • Check how much money you had on a specific day in past

How much is my trade profitable?

Every time you make a trade you are betting that the asset you receive will outperform the asset you give. It's easy to keep track of just one trade, but how much are your trades profitable after a couple of months, or years?

  • Compare how your trade is performing over time as opposed to holding the existing asset instead
  • Analyze details of every trade you made to get a better perspective of your trading strategy
  • Get total profit/loss for all trades in a given time period

How does my current portfolio compare to past portfolios?

We also keep track of your previous portfolios. By comparing past portfolios with your current portfolio, you can understand how much is your trading strategy giving results.

  • Compare your current portfolio to the best and worst portfolio in a given time period
  • Get insight into your overall trading strategy - are you a trader or a hodler?
